Introduction
Welcome to the Total Property API, your gateway to the UK property market’s dedicated home for private rented sector products and services. Total Property is the new, unified platform from the HFIS group, designed to bring all our award-winning brands together to support the entire tenancy lifecycle. This powerful integration simplifies processes for agents, landlords, and developers.

Our Total Property API provides a robust, single point of integration for our services. Phase one successfully launched with our mydeposits custodial deposit protection scheme. Phase two will see the inclusion of our insured-backed scheme, with all our property brands to follow in subsequent updates. This phased rollout ensures a stable and scalable environment.

By integrating with our secure and well-documented API, developers, online platforms, and agents can unlock streamlined operational capabilities. You can efficiently manage property portfolios, safeguard tenancy deposits, and handle the deposit release process through one seamless connection. Explore our sandbox environment to test your integration and rely on our dedicated developer support to guide you through the process, ensuring a swift, secure, and effective connection to our expanding ecosystem.

Requirements
To use the API requires familiarity with software development and web services. Requests and responses are in JSON format.

Supported Schemes
The API currently supports the following schemes:

England & Wales Custodial
Support for the following schemes will be added in a future version of the API:

England & Wales Insurance
Scotland Custodial

Authentication
The Total Property REST API uses the OAuth 2.0 framework to allow users of applications to securely access data without having to reveal username and password credentials to the third-party application.

To authenticate using OAuth, you will need a Consumer Key and Consumer Secret from mydeposits. Please send requests to businessdevelopment@mydeposits.co.uk. With your request, please include the following details:

Company Name

Contact name and email information

Consumer Type (Member of Software Company)

Agent or Landlord ID (If you are a member of Total Property)

Application Name

Sandbox 2FA Authentication
During 2FA on sandbox, users should always provide passcode '1111' as the OTP whenever prompted.
